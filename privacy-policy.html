Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 15, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Universal Calculator Suite. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our calculator tools.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide our services:

  • Calculation Data: The numbers and values you input into our calculators
  • Usage Data: Which calculators you use and when
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, and screen resolution
  • Local Storage: Your calculator preferences and recent calculations (stored locally on your device)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and improve our calculator tools
  • Remember your preferences between sessions
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Prevent abuse and ensure service security

4. Data Storage and Security

All calculations are processed locally in your browser. We use:

  • LocalStorage: Stores your preferences on your device only
  • SessionStorage: Temporary storage cleared when you close the browser
  • No Server Storage: We don't store your calculation data on our servers

5. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services that may collect information:

  • Google AdSense: Shows relevant advertisements and uses cookies for ad personalization
  • Analytics: We may use analytics tools to understand usage patterns

These services have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing them.

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to:

  • Remember your theme preference (dark/light mode)
  • Track which calculators you've used recently
  • Improve our service through anonymous analytics

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not work properly.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Clear your local storage data at any time
  • Disable cookies in your browser
  • Stop using our services
  • Contact us with privacy concerns

9. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary:

  • Local storage data persists until you clear it
  • Analytics data is anonymized and retained for up to 24 months
  • No personal calculation data is retained on our servers

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: privacy@universalcalculatorsuite.com
  • Website: https://yourdomain.com/contact.html
